Informed Consent

You are invited to participate in a research project conducted by Laura Kertz at the Cognitive, Linguistic, and Psychological Sciences Department, Brown University. This study investigates how people process and produce language.

If you decide to participate, you will be presented with questions and asked to answer them. Along with this, you may be asked about your language experience and background. At the end of the experiment, we will provide more information about what we are studying and the goals of our research.

All responses will be entered into a computer data file as anonymous responses. Your data will be assigned a numerical code, and there will be no code list linking your data to any of your personal information.

This experiment will take [3-30] minutes to complete and you will receive [$0.30- $3.00] per HIT completed as compensation.

You will not receive any direct benefits for participating in this study.

Risks in this study are minimal. You may feel some slight boredom or restlessness from answering similar questions for an extended period.

Please understand that your participation in this study is entirely voluntary and you have the right to withdraw your consent or discontinue participation for any reason at any time without penalty and without prejudice.

If you have any questions regarding this study, you may contact Laura Kertz at kertzlab@brown.edu.
